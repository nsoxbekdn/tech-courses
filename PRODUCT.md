# Product

## Register

brand (primary surface = the marketing/landing + catalog; app surfaces like the player and dashboard follow product discipline)

## Users
Indian Chartered Accountancy aspirants preparing for the ICAI exams (Foundation, Intermediate, Final). Mostly 17–25, studying long hours on laptop and phone, price-sensitive but quality-driven, choosing where to spend serious study time and money. They evaluate trust and rigour before paying.

## Product Purpose
A paid video-course platform for the full CA journey: browse subject courses, preview lessons, enrol and pay (Razorpay), then learn with progress tracking. Success = a visitor trusts the platform enough to enrol, and a student returns daily to study.

## Brand Personality
Precise, composed, credible. The voice of a good examiner: exact, calm, never hype. Three words: **precise, premium, professional.** It should feel like a well-set financial document, not a flashy ed-tech ad.

## Anti-references
- **Navy-blue + gold "fintech/finance" cliché** (the original look) — generic, reads as AI default.
- Loud ed-tech (Byju's-style gradients, confetti, urgency theatrics, rainbow course cards).
- Editorial-magazine costume (display-serif italics + drop caps + ruled columns) — a saturated cliché, wrong register here.
- Glassmorphism, glow-heavy heroes, bubbly oversized rounded corners.

## Design Principles
1. **Precision over decoration.** Hairlines, tabular figures, exact alignment. Every rule and number is deliberate, like a balance sheet that ties.
2. **Earn trust by restraint.** Calm surfaces and accurate copy signal a serious institution; we don't shout discounts.
3. **One confident accent.** A single deep green carries the brand; the rest is ink on paper. Color is a signal, not wallpaper.
4. **Readable for the long haul.** Built for hours of study: high contrast, generous measure, no eye strain.
5. **Sharp, not soft.** Tight radii and clean edges read as professional tooling, not a toy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA: body text ≥4.5:1, large text/UI ≥3:1, visible focus rings, full keyboard support. Honour `prefers-reduced-motion` (motion degrades to crossfade/instant). Don't rely on color alone (icons + text on states). Bilingual content reality (English + Hindi) — keep type and layout tolerant of longer strings.
